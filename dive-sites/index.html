One of the many Moalboal Dive Sites are Pescador Island & Sardine Run which are known for its beautiful & diverse marine life, & amazing school of sardines. Since you are on this page, know more about the other dive sites that are noteworthy checking out.

Scuba Diving in Moalboal Dive Sites

Pescador Island

  • District: Basdiot, Moalboal
  • Distance: 4km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 11min by banca boat GPS
  • Coordinates: N.09° 55.302′ E.123° 20.617′ Is south entry point N.09° 55.410′ E.123° 20.641′ Is east entry point
  • Composition: North and west deep wall, steep slope in the south. The Cathedral in northwest.
  • Snorkeling: Excellent
  • Experience: Open Water Dive Course
  • Diver Visibility: 10-30m
  • Current: 0-3 knots Interesting depth: 7-20m
  • Marine life: 450 spieces of hard coral, pyramid butterflyfish, tuna, nudibranches, snake eel, frog fish, barracuda and white tipped reef shark.

Neptune Diving Adventures House Reef (Sardine Run)

  • District: Basdiot, Moalboal
  • Distance: 0km
  • Travel Time: 0min by banca boat,beach entry
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 57.013′ E.123° 21.903′
  • Composition: Slope / wall.
  • Snorkeling: Good
  • Experience: Open Water Dive Course
  • Visibility: 10-20m
  • Current: 0-2 knots
  • Interesting depth: 5-15m
  • Marine life: Rich variety of hard and soft corals and of marine creatures. School of sardines being hunted, turtles, nudibranches, batfish and whaleshark.

Photos from Moalboal Sardine Run

Talisay Wall

  • District: Basdiot, Moalboal
  • Distance: 1km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 3min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 56.552′ E.123° 22.064′
  • Composition: Slope / wall, with canyons and small caves.
  • Snorkeling: Good
  • Experience: Open Water Dive Course
  • Visibility: 10-25m
  • Current: 0-3 knots
  • Interesting depth: 5-15m
  • Marine life: Turtles, batfish, mandarin fish and frogfish.

Tongo Point

  • District: Tongo, Moalboal
  • Distance: 2km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 7min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 56.063′ E.123° 22.376′ Entry point N.09° 55.936′ E.123° 22.587′ Exit point
  • Composition: Deep wall
  • Snorkeling: Good
  • Experience: Open Water Diver
  • Visibility: 10-25m
  • Current: 0-2 knots
  • Interesting depth: 5-18m
  • Marine life: Lots of caves and crevices with different nudibranches and shells. Turtle, clownfish and mandarinfish.

Kasai Point

  • District: Tuble, Moalboal
  • Distance: 1km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 3min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 57.532′ E.123° 22.024′
  • Composition: Steep slope with wall. Canyons and small caves.
  • Snorkeling: Excellent
  • Experience: Open Water Dive Course
  • Visibility: 10-22m
  • Current: 0-2 knots
  • Interesting depth: 7-15m
  • Marine life: Big hard corals, plenty of nudibranches, coral shrimpfish (razorfish), whaleshark and squid.

White Beach

  • District: Savedra, Moalboal
  • Distance: 5km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 13min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 59.583′ E.123° 22.180′ Entry point N.09° 59.451′ E.123° 22.160′ Exit point
  • Composition: Steep slope with wall.
  • Snorkeling: Good Experience: Open Water Dive Course
  • Visibility: 10-22m
  • Current: 0-3 knots
  • Interesting depth: 12-18m
  • Marine life: Soft corals and sea fans. Pelagic fish, longnose hawkfish, ghost pipefish and scorpion fish.

Ronda Bay

  • District: Savedra, Moalboal
  • Distance: 7km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 19min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 59.750′ E.123° 23.000′
  • Composition: Slope
  • Snorkeling: Perfect
  • Experience: Open Water Dive Course
  • Visibility: 8-18m Current: 0-2 knots
  • Interesting depth: 10-15m
  • Marine life: Sea pen, spotted eagle ray, blacktipped reefshark and batfish. Plenty of different nudibranches.

Dolphin House

  • District: Savedra, Moalboal
  • Distance: 2.4km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 8min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 58.281′ E.123° 22.195′
  • Composition: Steep slope with wall.
  • Snorkeling: Good
  • Experience: Open Water Dive Course
  • Visibility: 10-25m Current: 0-2
  • Interesting depth: 17-22m
  • Marine life: Very big sea fans, turtle, wahoo, longnose hawkfish and nudibranches.

Sunken Island

  • District: Lambug, Badian
  • Distance: 10,4km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 26min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 51.524′ E.123° 20.654′
  • Composition: Shoal hill
  • Snorkeling: Not good
  • Experience: Advanced Open Water Course Advanced Open Water Course
  • Visibility: 12-30m Current: 1-4 knots Interesting depth: 25-30m. Marine life: Underwater island with pelagic schools of fish. Frogfish, big lionfish, and spanish dancer.

Airplane Wreck

  • District: Savedra, Moalboal
  • Distance: 6km from Panagsama Beach
  • Travel Time: 11min by banca boat
  • GPS Coordinates: N.09° 59.872′ E.123° 22.425′
  • Composition: Slope / wall
  • Snorkeling: Perfect
  • Experience: Advanced Open Water Course
  • Visibility: 5-20m Current: 1-3 knots
  • Interesting depth: 10-18m, airplane at 22m
  • Marine life: Lots of different corals, spotted eagle ray, yellow ribbon sweetlips, ringtailed cardinal, majestic angelfish, mantis shrimp and squid.
