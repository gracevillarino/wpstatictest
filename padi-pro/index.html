Are you ready to join the ranks of the dedicated professionals who teach the world’s most progressive and popular scuba diver education programs?

Have you ever thought of a Career change? Want to have a office with sand in between toes instead of in your dreams?

Neptune Diving Adventure is a PADI 5 Star IDC Center who are teaching everything from PADI Open Water to PADI Professionals in conjunction with IDC Philippines and our Multi Award wining Platinum PADI Course Director Camille Lemmens who is personally teaching all professional courses him self with help from our Staff Instructors.

All of our Candidates are getting Nitrox and Oxygene Provider Instructor course for free (Not application) when they are joining us for our IDCs.

Most of our Candidates choose to do our MSDT package during their IDC, You will get to teach a minimum of 5 specialties right after the IE when you have completed you speciality training with us. You will also be a lot more attractive when you are going to apply for your first job as a PADI Instructor.

Neptune Diving Adventure is the First and Leading PADI 5 Star IDC Center on Cebu, Philippines and after more then 20 years in the business still going strong with minimum 4 IDCs scheduled over the year.
